## Core Features:

- Hero Section: Eye-catching hero section with a clear title, subtitle, eBook cover image, and a compelling call to action.
- Social Proof Carousel: Dynamic carousel displaying short testimonials with photos, showcasing real results from users to build trust and credibility.
- Benefits Showcase: Highlight the transformative benefits of the eBook through visually appealing cards with icons and concise descriptions, focusing on user outcomes.
- Content Overview: Clearly present the eBook's content with a bullet-point list, enhanced by relevant icons, providing a transparent overview of what users will receive.
- Upsell Order Bumps: Streamlined order bump options offering related products with checkboxes/mini buttons. This feature enables users to easily add items, dynamically updating the total value using a tool before redirecting them to the Kiwify checkout.
- Nutri Batata Profile: Concise author section featuring a circular photo of Nutri Batata and a brief bio, reinforcing expertise and personal connection.
- Sticky CTA Footer: A persistent 'Quero meu eBook' button anchored to the bottom of the mobile screen. Provides immediate access to the product, increasing conversion rates through continuous visibility.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Soft olive green (#5E7C64) for a clean, comforting, and professional aesthetic.
- Background color: Light beige (#FAF8F6) to provide a clean and neutral backdrop.
- Accent color: Gentle orange (#E58E50) to highlight calls to action and important elements.
- Body text font: 'Inter' sans-serif for clear and legible paragraphs.
- Headline font: 'Montserrat' sans-serif for strong titles.
- Use clear and simple icons that represent benefits and content, in the style of UX icons from Phosphor or Tabler.
- Mobile-first layout with single-column design and generous spacing to ensure optimal readability and user experience on mobile devices.
- Use subtle fade-in animations for cards as the user scrolls, adding a smooth, engaging feel to the interface.
